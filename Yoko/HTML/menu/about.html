The business is a restaurant that not only provides food and drinks but also offers courses and catering services. It is a unique dining experience that combines the pleasures of good food with the opportunity to learn more about cooking and food preparation. Customers can enjoy a delicious meal while also taking part in cooking classes and workshops to develop their culinary skills. In addition to the restaurant and courses, the business also offers catering services for events and special occasions, providing a diverse range of cuisine options to meet the needs of any event. The business aims to create a welcoming and enjoyable atmosphere for its customers, providing them with a complete dining experience that goes beyond just a meal. The restaurant also sources high-quality, fresh ingredients, ensuring that the food is of the highest standard and always prepared to perfection. Overall, the business provides a unique and immersive dining experience that caters to customers looking for both good food and a fun, educational experience.
